COFOG Enterprise Payroll FAQ (Sample Dataset)

Disclaimer: This is a fictional enterprise payroll handbook created for
training, RAG, and AI demonstration purposes.

==================================================================== 1.
What is the Enterprise Payroll Team responsible for?
====================================================================

The Enterprise Payroll Team is responsible for processing employee
salaries accurately and on time. The team manages payroll calculations,
statutory deductions, tax deductions, leave deductions, reimbursements,
incentives, overtime payments, bonus processing, final settlements, and
compliance with local labor regulations.

Payroll processing follows a predefined monthly calendar. Multiple
validation checkpoints ensure employee master data, attendance, leave
balances, and salary revisions are verified before salary disbursement.

==================================================================== 2.
When is salary credited?
====================================================================

Employees are generally paid on the last working day of every month. If
the last working day falls on a weekend or public holiday, salary is
credited on the previous working day.

The payroll calendar is communicated at the beginning of every financial
year.

==================================================================== 3.
What components are included in salary?
====================================================================

A typical salary structure consists of:

-   Basic Pay
-   House Rent Allowance (HRA)
-   Special Allowance
-   Conveyance Allowance
-   Medical Allowance
-   Performance Incentive
-   Shift Allowance
-   Bonus
-   Overtime
-   Employer PF Contribution
-   Employer Insurance Contribution

Certain components vary depending on employee grade, location, and
employment type.

==================================================================== 4.
What deductions appear in my payslip?
====================================================================

Common deductions include:

-   Provident Fund (PF)
-   Professional Tax (PT)
-   Employee State Insurance (ESI) where applicable
-   Income Tax (TDS)
-   Leave Without Pay (LWP)
-   Recovery of salary advances
-   Cafeteria deductions
-   Insurance premiums
-   Other statutory deductions

Every deduction includes a description in the monthly payslip.

==================================================================== 5.
What should I do if my salary is incorrect?
====================================================================

Employees should raise a Payroll Support Ticket through the HR Service
Portal within seven working days of salary credit.

The ticket should include:

• Employee ID • Pay Month • Description of the issue • Supporting
documents • Screenshot of payslip (if applicable)

The payroll team investigates the issue, validates payroll records, and
communicates the resolution.

==================================================================== 6.
Why was Leave Without Pay deducted?
====================================================================

Leave Without Pay (LWP) is applied when approved leave balance is
insufficient or attendance records indicate unpaid absence.

The payroll engine automatically calculates salary deductions based on
the number of unpaid leave days.

==================================================================== 7.
How is overtime calculated?
====================================================================

Eligible employees receive overtime payment based on approved overtime
hours submitted by their reporting manager.

Only approved overtime before payroll cutoff is included in the current
month’s salary.

Late approvals are processed in the next payroll cycle.

==================================================================== 8.
What is the payroll cutoff date?
====================================================================

Payroll cutoff is generally the 20th of every month.

Changes submitted after the cutoff may be processed in the following
payroll cycle.

Examples include:

-   Promotion
-   Salary revision
-   Bank account update
-   Tax declaration
-   Attendance corrections

==================================================================== 9.
How are bonuses processed?
====================================================================

Performance bonuses are processed separately after approval by business
leadership.

Bonus calculations consider:

-   Performance Rating
-   Business Unit Performance
-   Organization Performance
-   Employment Status
-   Eligibility Period

Applicable taxes are deducted before payment.

==================================================================== 10.
What happens during Full and Final Settlement?
====================================================================

When an employee leaves the organization, the Payroll Team processes
Full and Final Settlement after receiving clearance from HR, Finance,
IT, and Administration.

The settlement may include:

-   Pending salary
-   Leave encashment
-   Bonus eligibility
-   Recoveries
-   Gratuity (if applicable)
-   Tax adjustments

Processing normally completes within statutory timelines.

==================================================================== 11.
Why is TDS deducted?
====================================================================

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is calculated based on projected annual
taxable income, submitted investment declarations, exemptions, and
prevailing tax regulations.

Employees should review tax declarations before the payroll cutoff.

==================================================================== 12.
How do I download my payslip?
====================================================================

Employees can log in to the Employee Self-Service Portal and navigate
to:

Payroll → Payslips → Financial Year → Select Month → Download PDF

Payslips remain available for historical reference.

==================================================================== 13.
Can I change my bank account?
====================================================================

Yes.

Employees must submit a Bank Account Change Request before payroll
cutoff.

Supporting documents:

-   Cancelled Cheque
-   Bank Passbook
-   Official Bank Statement

Payroll validates the request before updating employee records.

==================================================================== 14.
Who should I contact for payroll support?
====================================================================

Employees should raise requests through the HR Service Portal rather
than sending emails directly.

Typical ticket categories include:

-   Missing Salary
-   Incorrect Salary
-   Tax Query
-   Bonus Query
-   Bank Account Change
-   Payslip Request
-   PF Query
-   Final Settlement Query

==================================================================== 15.
How is payroll data secured?
====================================================================

Payroll data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Access is
controlled through role-based permissions. All payroll activities are
logged for auditing. Sensitive employee information is accessible only
to authorized payroll personnel. Backup and disaster recovery procedures
ensure payroll continuity.

End of Sample Payroll FAQ
